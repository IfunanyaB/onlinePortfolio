Introduction
After completing my Udemy course on web design, I wanted to dive deeper into UI/UX by working on my first project: wireframing and prototyping my portfolio using Figma.

In creating this portfolio/website, I focused on selecting the right component libraries, choosing typography and a color palette, and designing the interactions I wanted for my site.

My target audience includes employers, managers, and job recruiters in the UI/UX design field.
