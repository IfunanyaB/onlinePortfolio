 bp's Digital Design & UX on Forage

Overview
I took part in BP’s job simulation on the Forage platform, which provided valuable insights into the experience of working within a digital UX design team. My project focused on researching and developing a single user flow for an  an app tailored for the electric vehicle (EV) industry. BP’s commitment to leading the sustainable energy transition is evident in its recognition of electric vehicles as a key component of the future.
 
 
Onboarding
I was assigned to conduct user research aimed at helping EV users find and navigate to the nearest charging station. To gain a deeper understanding, I explored popular EV apps, analyzed their features, and reviewed user feedback. This research was instrumental in shaping the user persona detailed below.

I proceeded with creating single user-flow low-fi wireframes to help me concentrate on the basic structure and layout of a design without getting distracted by details like colors, fonts, or images.

I then moved on to creating high-fidelity wireframes, allowing me to focus on the finer details of the design, such as colors, fonts, and imagery, while refining the overall structure and layout.
